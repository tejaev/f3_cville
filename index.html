For New Guys

How to show up for your first workout.

You do not need to be in shape, know anybody, or understand the jargon. Just show up a few minutes early, tell a guy it is your first workout, and follow along at your own pace.

01

Pick A Workout

Use the schedule on this page and choose a location that fits your morning.

02

Bring The Basics

Wear workout clothes, bring water and gloves, and expect to be outside the full time.

03

Say That You’re New

We call you an FNG, short for Friendly New Guy. Somebody will take it from there.

04

Stay For COT

The Circle of Trust closes every workout and helps you get connected quickly.

PAX / Q

Leadership gets built one workout at a time.

In F3, the Q is the man leading the workout. Effective leadership starts with preparation, accountability, and bringing energy without making the workout about yourself.

  • Start and finish on time. No exceptions.
  • Plan the workout, then scale it for every fitness level.
  • Demonstrate movements clearly and keep the group moving.
  • Protect the culture: welcome new guys, encourage guys lagging behind, and close in COT.
