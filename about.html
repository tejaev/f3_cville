Mission

Our mission is to plant, grow and serve small workout groups for men for the invigoration of male community leadership.

That mission matters because too many men drift into isolation, passivity, or shallow routine. F3 uses free, peer-led workouts as the first point of contact, but the goal is much bigger than physical training.

The point is to build men who lead well at home, work hard in the community, and refuse to coast through life alone.
